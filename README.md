# ResuMatch – AI-Powered Applicant Tracking System (ATS)

ResuMatch is a MERN Stack-based AI-powered Applicant Tracking System designed to simplify the recruitment process for both candidates and recruiters.

Candidates can upload their resumes, analyze resume standards, browse job openings, and apply for jobs. Recruiters can create and manage job postings, review applications, and use AI-powered resume parsing and keyword matching to identify the most suitable candidates.

The system automatically extracts information from resumes, compares applicant skills and keywords with job requirements, calculates ATS match scores, and ranks applicants based on compatibility. Recruiters can then review detailed candidate profiles, shortlist applicants, and manage the hiring process efficiently.

## Key Features

- Candidate Registration & Login
- Recruiter Registration & Login
- Resume Upload & Resume Standards Analysis
- Job Posting & Job Management
- Job Applications Management
- AI Resume Parsing
- Keyword-Based Candidate Matching
- ATS Match Score Calculation
- Candidate Ranking
- Shortlist & Under Review Status
- Recruiter & Candidate Dashboards
- Profile Management

## Tech Stack

- Frontend: React.js
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js
- Database: MongoDB
- AI/Parsing: Python, PyPDF2 / python-docx
